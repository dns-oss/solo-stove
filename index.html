3. Direct-to-Consumer Strengthening

  • Enhance D2C experience with exclusive bundles unavailable through retail.
  • Develop loyalty programs that reward direct purchases.
  • Implement customer education campaigns highlighting authentic Solo Stove experience.

5. Market Communication Strategy

  • Develop clear brand messaging emphasizing product quality and experience over price.
  • Establish a consistent seasonal promotional calendar to manage retailer expectations.

2. Value-Added Program Structure

  • Develop co-marketing programs that reward brand-supportive behavior.
  • Offer exclusive products or early access to retailers maintaining price integrity.
  • Create incentive structures tied to full-price sell-through rather than volume alone.

4. Supply Chain Management

  • Implement more granular inventory allocation based on retailer performance.
  • Monitor and analyze sell-through data to identify optimal inventory distribution.

6. Monitoring & Analytics Enhancement

  • Implement real-time price monitoring across all channels.
  • Develop retailer scorecards combining pricing behavior, stock management, and brand presentation.
